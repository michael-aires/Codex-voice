Weekly product brief

Thursday, July 16

What changed

We shipped the bulk export improvement and rolled out the new invite flow to 25% of users.

  • Bulk export now includes filters and saved views
  • Invite flow conversion is up 12% in the early cohort
  • Fixed the role-permission issue on shared folders

What we learned

Users value speed and clarity in data exports more than custom formatting.

Key insight

Simpler export options lead to higher completion and fewer support tickets.

Decisions

  • Roll out the invite flow to 100% next week
  • Defer custom export formatting to Q3
  • Prioritize performance tuning for large datasets

Next week

We will focus on performance improvements and begin user testing for the dashboard refresh. Research sessions are scheduled with five customers.
