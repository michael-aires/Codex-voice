## Demo interpretation

This artifact is an illustrative product demo of the white paper's proposed session-review experience. It is not evidence that the complete workflow is deployed. The UI combines source-grounded behaviors into a coherent walkthrough:

1. Start a session from Today.
2. Define its purpose, account, project, and participants.
3. Select authorized context from Notion, GitHub, Zoom, or manual sources.
4. Work with Cooper in a collaborator rail and evidence canvas.
5. Create a reviewable document during the session.
6. Preview and approve the exact destination write.
7. End the live interaction and synthesize the session.
8. Confirm decisions, actions, commitments, and open questions.
9. Close with traceable artifacts and source-backed memory.
