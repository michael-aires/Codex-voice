- Data rules: all statements are framed as the white paper's proposed operating model; date and version match the source.
- Design rules: approved AIRES palette, Urbanist/Inter/Instrument Serif/IBM Plex Mono, restrained geometry, 8 px rhythm, sharp corners, and sparse Volt accent.
- Forbidden shortcuts: no public deployment, local screenshot presented as customer proof, unauthenticated customer URL, generic template claims, shipped-feature implication, or fabricated metric.
- Non-goals: validating Zoom/Notion/GitHub API details, proving current product implementation, publishing to Vercel, sending a Slack message, or producing a customer case study.
