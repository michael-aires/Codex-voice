# Aries Company OS — concept film working page

> Internal source-grounded video brief. Source: *Aries Company OS*, version 1.0, July 13, 2026.

## Audience and intent

The film gives product, customer-success, and engineering leaders a concise guided tour of the Company OS thesis. It describes a proposed operating model, not a shipped customer workflow.

## Narrative

1. **Work begins before the ticket.** Conversation contains evidence and nuance that systems of record can lose.
2. **Session equals durable context.** A source-grounded workspace links participants, decisions, approvals, delivery, and proof.
3. **Signal to evidence to session.** Zoom is the first proposed ingestion path; the domain remains source-neutral.
4. **Preparation is automated. Authority is not.** Agents distill proposals without creating commitments.
5. **Human judgment at every gate.** Cooper supports challenge, revision, scoping, and explicit approval.
6. **Approved to delivered to verified.** Canonical work, implementation, and QA evidence stay connected.
7. **Every outcome returns to context.** The next conversation begins from verified history.
8. **Aries Company OS.** A context compiler between Zoom, Notion, GitHub, and QA.

## Validation contract

- Every scene maps to the local source ledger.
- The script avoids implementation, efficacy, compliance, and customer-outcome overclaims.
- Storyboard HTML must pass desktop and narrow-width visual checks before video render.
- Representative stills and final media metadata must be verified after render.
