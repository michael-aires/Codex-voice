## Uncertainty and guardrails

- The white paper is a concept and strategy source, not independent evidence of implementation or efficacy.
- Appendix A separates present repository foundations from proposed additions. The film does not claim the full workflow is production-ready.
- The Zoom-first path is proposed and depends on account settings, authorization, available assets, and security controls.
- Roadmap phases are plans, not completed milestones.
- Success metrics are definitions, not reported results.
- Trust, consent, retention, isolation, and prompt-injection controls are requirements, not compliance claims.
- The illustrative first workflow is not presented as a customer case study.
- The onboarding chapter is a synthesis of the proposed session type, account-memory model, and Phase 4 roadmap; it is visibly labeled `Proposed workflow`.
