## 1. 01 / The operating problem: Work begins before the ticket.

The most important software work often begins in conversation. But as it moves into tickets, requirements, code, and QA, the original evidence and nuance can disappear.

## 2. 02 / The thesis: Session = durable context

Aries Company OS proposes a durable session: a source-grounded workspace connecting context, participants, decisions, approvals, and downstream work. Larger than a call, smaller than a project.

## 3. 03 / First signal: Signal → Evidence → Session

In the first proposed workflow, a completed Zoom meeting triggers authorized retrieval of its summary or transcript, creation of a source ledger, and a review-ready session.

## 4. 04 / Distillation: Preparation is automated. Authority is not.

Background agents segment and corroborate the evidence, then draft decisions, actions, requests, and bugs. These are proposals, not commitments. Preparation is automated. Authority is not.

## 5. 05 / Concrete workflow: Bug triage: evidence before severity.

A customer reports an intermittent workflow bug during a product review. Aries drafts an evidence-anchored bug proposal; the manager confirms impact but leaves severity open. Product commits it to the active sprint, engineering links the pull request, and Sentinel records a passing, evidence-backed verdict before Cooper prepares the customer update.

## 6. 06 / Concrete workflow: Feature request: shape the outcome. Then scope.

The same review surfaces a reporting gap. Aries drafts the user job, evidence, expected outcome, constraints, and open discovery questions. The manager rejects an inferred commitment and edits the outcome, then Cooper runs the Requirements Framework interview. Scoped requirements and a Notion preview are approved before product sends the request to backlog.

## 7. 07 / Proposed workflow: Customer onboarding: every milestone remembers.

For customer onboarding, a proposed session starts from the kickoff call, implementation plan, and Notion page. Aries assembles goals, owners, milestones, integration dependencies, risks, and blockers into a review brief. The customer-success lead confirms commitments and next actions; approved updates return to source-backed account memory for the next milestone review.

## 8. 08 / Governance: Human judgment at every gate

Across each workflow, Cooper presents the evidence visually and by voice. A human can challenge an inference, merge or reject proposals, refine requirements, and explicitly approve each consequential step.

## 9. 09 / Delivery loop: Approved → Delivered → Verified

Approved work can be previewed and written to Notion, linked to GitHub delivery, and verified by evidence-backed QA against its acceptance criteria.

## 10. 10 / Organizational memory: Every outcome returns to context.

Delivery evidence returns to the session and customer memory, so the next meeting begins from verified history instead of reconstructed memory.

## 11. 11 / The promise: Company OS

Aries does not replace Zoom, Notion, GitHub, or QA. It compiles context between them: turning organizational conversation into governed, traceable, and verifiably completed work.
